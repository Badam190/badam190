Literature

She loves to read.

That is all she packs.

One day I will have this many books.

Her best friend is Christopher Marlowe, who was an English playwright, poet and translator of the Elizabethan era. He wrote Doctor Faustus, which is a name that Adam uses when he goes for blood at the hospital. "I do wish I'd met Adam before I wrote Hamlet, he would have made a marvellous model," Marlowe tells Eve.

"Hell is just a frame of mind." - Christopher Marlowe, Doctor Faustus
